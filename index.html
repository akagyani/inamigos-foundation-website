Who We Are

Empowering Communities Since 2020

Founded by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog listed non-profit driven by the philosophy of "Service Before Self".

Our Story

What began as a localized effort in Chhattisgarh during a time of global crisis has blossomed into a nationwide volunteer movement. Operating as a recognized Section 8 non-profit with 12A & 80G tax benefits, we unite professionals and youth across the country to deliver direct, transparent, and high-impact welfare programs.

We believe that real change happens when local communities are empowered to take ownership of their development. Through structured flagship projects, we address the critical pillars of human and environmental growth.

Our Vision

A more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society driven by collective action where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and live with dignity.

Our Mission

To pioneer sustainable social development through targeted initiatives in hunger alleviation, child education, women empowerment, stray animal care, and environmental sustainability.

Our Core Values

Compassion

Empathy is at the heart of everything we do. We serve with love and respect.

Transparency

We are accountable to our donors, volunteers, and the communities we serve.

Collective Action

We believe in the power of united minds and collaborative efforts to scale impact.

Service Before Self

Our commitment is centered purely on the growth and happiness of others.

What We Do

Our Flagship Projects

We tackle key humanitarian and developmental challenges through six structured projects across communities in India.

Humanitarian

Project SEVA

Direct food and clothing security for daily wage workers, street dwellers, and underprivileged families facing severe economic hardships.

Objectives:
  • Alleviate immediate hunger through fresh meal drives.
  • Distribute clean clothes and winter blankets.
  • Establish localized community food banks.
100K+ Meals Served
Education

Project Bachpanshala

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children through community classes, digital literacy, and holistic mentorship.

Objectives:
  • Provide basic academic coaching and literacy support.
  • Introduce digital learning tools and computer basics.
  • Distribute school kits, books, and writing supplies.
5,000+ Children Mentored
Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Fostering financial independence for underprivileged women through vocational skill training, rights awareness, and entrepreneurship support.

Objectives:
  • Conduct professional tailoring and handicraft courses.
  • Facilitate workshops on financial planning and bank access.
  • Educate on health, hygiene, and constitutional rights.
1,200+ Women Empowered
Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Ensuring the safety, nourishment, and medical well-being of stray animals, building an empathetic local community.

Objectives:
  • Coordinate daily feeding drives for stray dogs and cattle.
  • Provide emergency medical first aid and rescue assistance.
  • Promote stray animal sterilization and adoption drives.
15K+ Animals Supported
Environment

Project Prakriti

Combatting climate change and urban degradation through green cover expansion and smart waste management programs.

Objectives:
  • Execute large-scale native tree plantation drives.
  • Promote clean-ups and plastic-free marketplace campaigns.
  • Conduct waste segregation and composting workshops.
10K+ Saplings Planted
Skill Dev

Project Vikas

Preparing underprivileged youth and students for careers through professional skills, resume workshops, and internship programs.

Objectives:
  • Build capabilities in soft skills, communication, and digital literacy.
  • Deliver structured internship roles inside the NGO framework.
  • Organize career counseling sessions and mentorship links.
3,000+ Interns Trained
